Our Story

G3 Accounting Limited was established in 2014 with a vision to provide world-class accounting and business consulting services in Papua New Guinea.

As a 100% locally-owned firm, we understand the unique challenges and opportunities that businesses face in our region. Our team of experienced professionals is dedicated to helping our clients navigate complex financial landscapes.

Over the years, we have grown to serve hundreds of clients across various industries, from small businesses to large corporations, always maintaining our commitment to excellence and integrity.
